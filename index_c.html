Surah Al-Fatiha


Heledzina la he MWGNI YEZI, M'Nemesha Douniani, na Ahira 1[ Verset1/Page1 ] (1)

Wowu tukufu wahe MWIGNI YEZI, Mlezi wahe ziwumbe(2). Ye Mzi Nemesha Douniani , na Ahira(3), Ye Mfoma he kiyama(4). Wendo rihu anbuduwo, Wendo rihu tsaho m'saanda(5). Ridundzé ndziya ya wokofu(6), Yeyaho wa wanemesha, Wuri pukane ne yaho wa hundro wusiwu waho, ne yaho wangamiya 2[ Verset7/Page1 ] (7)
